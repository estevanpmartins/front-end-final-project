How to play the Memory game
  • The cards are in paired ,if their 16 cards showing on screen it mean their are only 8 card images in hidden mode and they are repected to make 16 cards . It required to win only on 8 click and result will be positive. If number of click greater than number of unique cards then result will be negative and this rule will be same for 24 cards, 32 cards, 40 cards and 48 cards .
  • The cards are placed face down. Each card has a matching card.
  • For each turn, the player select two cards.
  • If the cards match, they are removed from the board.
  • If the cards don't match, they are turned face down again.
  • At the end of the game, the player receives a score based on the number of correct selections that were made.
